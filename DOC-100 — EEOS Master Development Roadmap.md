## One recommendation before continuing

Instead of keeping this only as a chat plan, create a new document in your EEOS Bible called:

**DOC-100 — EEOS Master Development Roadmap**

This should become the single source of truth for the project, with every item assigned a status (`Not Started`, `In Progress`, `Blocked`, `Testing`, `Completed`), priority, dependencies, and target milestone. It will serve as your project management backbone throughout development and beta.
