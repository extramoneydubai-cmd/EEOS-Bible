# Purpose

This document defines the complete lifecycle of a lead inside EEOS.

Every CRM feature, automation, task, dashboard, collection, admission, reporting and AI assistant must follow this lifecycle.

This is the official source of truth.

---

# Philosophy

A Lead is NOT just a name and phone number.

A Lead represents an entire family journey.

The objective is not to "close a sale".

The objective is

Enquiry

↓

Counselling

↓

Admission

↓

Student Success

↓

Alumni

↓

Referral

↓

Lifetime Relationship
